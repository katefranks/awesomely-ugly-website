About Victor's Pizza

Vicor Pizzatti opened his doors on May 17th, 1991. Greenville had a void: no one in the area was serving unique pizzas with interesting ingredients! Victor rose to the occasion and met his customer's demands! Thanks to Victor, weird pizzas are now a reality for all Pizzaville citizens, delivered directly to their doorstep!
